Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TechCon 2025

When and where is TechCon 2025?

+

TechCon 2025 will take place on March 15–17, 2025 at the Accra International Conference Centre, Ghana. The venue is easily accessible and centrally located.

Are there student or group discounts?

+

Yes! Students get 50% off with valid ID. Groups of 5+ automatically get 25% off per ticket. Contact us at hello@techcon.africa for larger teams.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

+

Full refunds are available until February 1, 2025. After that, tickets are transferable — you can give your ticket to a colleague or friend.

Will talks be recorded?

+

Yes! All main stage talks will be professionally recorded and made available to attendees within 2 weeks after the event.

Is the venue accessible?

+

Absolutely. The venue is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. Let us know your needs when registering.

Do you offer visa support letters?

+

Yes! Once you purchase a ticket, we’ll send you an official visa invitation letter to support your application.
